The vision of the Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible project is not only to provide resources, but to develop a unified system integrating four facets of biblical study.

  1. Starting with the text of the Hebrew Bible. Ours is marked up in OSIS XML.
  2. Looking up the Hebrew words in a Lexicon superior to Strongâ€™s dictionary.
  3. Interpreting Morphology data in a readable format.
  4. Visualizing connections and divisions within a verse, recorded by the Masoretes using Cantillation marks in the text.
